Settlement agreements

A settlement agreement is a legal agreement between you and the defendant describing how and when they will repay you.

If the defendant chooses to sign your agreement, the claim will be put on hold and you will not be able to issue a CCJ against them unless they break the agreement.

If they do not feel the repayment plan is affordable, they can ask a judge to determine a suitable plan.
