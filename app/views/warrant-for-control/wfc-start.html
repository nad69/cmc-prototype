Apply for a warrant of control

You can apply for a warrant of control if you have an money claim judgement but the defendant hasn’t paid. You can ask the County Court enforcement agent to try and get back any amount up to £5000.

A warrant of control will only help if the person who owes you money has:

  • Sufficient funds to pay all the money you are claiming for OR
  • Enough goods at the address you provide which could be sold at auction to raise money to pay your claim

What you'll need

To use this service you need:

  • The address of the person owing money that you want the enforcement agent to visit
  • Details of the claim amount and how you’d like the money be collected
