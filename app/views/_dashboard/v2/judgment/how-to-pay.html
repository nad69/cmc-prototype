How to pay a County Court Judgment (CCJ)

Contact the claimant and agree how to pay. For example by cheque or direct debit. You can't pay the court.

Use the claim number for payments such as:

  • bank transfers
  • receipts from the claimant if you pay by cheque

Keep a record of all payments made

Allow up to 4 days for your payment to reach the claimant.
