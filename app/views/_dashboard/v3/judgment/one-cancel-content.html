Apply to cancel the CCJ

You'll have to pay a court fee of £255. If you're on a low income you can get help with fees.

You'll need to explain why you couldn't respond. You may need to go to a hearing.

If your application is successful

Your CCJ will be removed from public records. In most cases you'll still have to respond to the claim.

The judge might ask the claimant to refund your fee of £255.

If your application is unsuccessful

You might have to pay the claimant's costs if it goes to a hearing. The CCJ will stay on the register for 6 years.
