Terms of the agreement

This agreement settles the claim made by Jan Clark against Mary Richards. This includes all money owed in the case, for example counterclaims, court fees, expenses or interest.

This agreement replaces the claim. Neither of you can make any further claims relating to this case.

This agreement is confidential until you've both signed it. After that it can be used in court, for example if the agreement is broken.

You can view and download this agreement at any time from your money claims account.

If the agreement is broken

Either of you can apply to:

  • request a judgment for any money still owed from this agreement
  • restart the original claim or counterclaim for the full amount of the claim

You should complete form N244 and send it to the County Court Business Centre. You need to say on the form which of the 2 options you're applying for.
