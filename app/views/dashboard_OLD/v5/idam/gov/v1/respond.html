3. Respond to a money claim

You need to respond to the claim within 14 days of receiving it. The exact deadline date is on the letter or email you received explaining the claim.

We'll send your response to the claimant (the person or organisation who says you owe them money).

If you don't respond

The claimant can apply for a County Court Judgment (CCJ) against you if you don't respond within 14 days of receiving the claim.

They can use a CCJ to get the money from you, for example, by asking the court to send bailiffs to your home or business.

A CCJ may make it harder to get credit, such as a mobile phone contract, credit card or mortgage.
