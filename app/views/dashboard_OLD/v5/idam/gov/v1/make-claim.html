2. Make a Money Claim

You can make a money claim if a person or organisation owes you money and won’t pay you back.

Before claiming you should contact the person or organisation to try to find a solution. Find out how you can resolve a claim by discussion or mediation.

Your claim, including your name and address, will be sent to the person you say owes you money. They'll get a chance to respond to your claim.

You might have to go to a hearing with a judge if the person says they don’t owe you money.

What you'll need

To use this service you need:

  • current address and contact details for the person or organisation you’re claiming against (or last known address)
  • a credit or debit card to pay the claim fee
  • 18 or older (you must also be claiming against someone who's 18 or older)
  • living in the UK (and claiming against a person or organisation with an address in England or Wales)
