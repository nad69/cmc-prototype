Make a money claim

You can make a money claim if you believe a person or organisation owes you money and won’t pay you back.

Before claiming you should contact the person or organisation to try to find a solution. Find out how you can resolve a claim by discussion or mediation.

Your claim, including your name and address, will be sent to the person you say owes you money. They'll get a chance to respond to your claim.

You might have to go to court if the person says they don’t owe you the money.
