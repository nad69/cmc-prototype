Completing your claim

You don't owe the money

You can apply to cancel the judgment if you didn't get or respond to the claim. This is called getting the judgment 'set aside'. You may have to pay a court fee of £255.

You'll need to explain in your application and at a private hearing why you weren't able to respond.

You can ask to change the way you pay.
