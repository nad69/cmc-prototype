Money claim rules

Before you make a claim

You must try to resolve the dispute directly before making a claim by:

  • Speaking to them (send an email or letter if you want a record).
  • Telling them what you believe they owe you and why.
  • Explaining that if you can't reach an agreement (such as a timetable for paying the money) you'll make a legal money claim.
  • Telling them they might have to pay the full amount as well as fees and interest if you claim, and that they could get a County Court Judgment (CCJ).

After you've claimed

Try to settle your case before a hearing even after you have submitted your claim to the court.

You can use our service to help you resolve your dispute and reach an agreement, if needed.
